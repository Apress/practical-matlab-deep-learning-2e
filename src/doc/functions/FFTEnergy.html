Form

[e, w, wP] = FFTEnergy( y, tSamp, aPeak )

Description

FFT of the input signal converted to energy. The resolution in frequency will depend on the number of samples and the sampling period.

Inputs

y     (1,n) Sequence
tSamp	(1,1) Sampling period
aPeak (1,1) Peak fraction

Outputs

e     (1,n)  Energy
w     (1,n)  Frequencies (rad/sec)
wP    (1,:)  Frequencies of peaks  (rad/sec)
