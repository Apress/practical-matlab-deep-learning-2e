Generate the quaternions that transform from ECI to LVLH coordinates.

For LVLH coordinates; z is in the -r direction y is in the - rxv direction x completes the set; along v in a circular orbit

Form:

q = QLVLH( r, v )

Inputs

r          (3,n) Position vectors
v          (3,n) Velocity vectors

Outputs

q          (4,n) Quaternions
